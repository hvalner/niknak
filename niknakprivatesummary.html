Our Message

Life is hard, complex, and stressful. Students are overburdened by the demands of the educational system and being an adult means lots of responsibility. At Nik Nak we aim to make life a little better by bringing our customers meaningful 3D printed gadgets to destress and focus. Our first line is a series fidget toys made specifically for USC and USC IYA Students.


High Level Concept

We aim to be a second to market company that rides off of the increased interest and popularity of fidget toys as can be seen with the latest Fidget Cube kickstarter campaign.


Product

We offer uniquely designed, readily available, low-cost spinner gadgets to our consumers. These spinners are designed by our in-house team and distributed on campus at the moment of purchase.
