For focus and fun: Scientific studies show that fidgeting drastically improves attention, critical thinking and creativity. We all know how hard it is to sit still and stay awake through class. With Niknak Fidget Spinners you can engage your hands and your mind with the simplest and most effective fidget device around.

How theyre made: Our Niknak Fidget Spinners utilize cutting edge manufacture technology to get the best product in your hands quickly. Made from incredibly durable, eco-friendly PLA plastic, they'll hold up to thousands of hours of spinning without showing wear. The spinners are 3D printed on top of the line commercial 3D printers, so that we can get the widest range of colors and shapes to you at the lowest cost possible. We use only the best bearings so that they'll give the best spinning epxerience.

How to use: Niknak Fidget Spinners are simple and intuitive, designed with the user in mind. While there's no wrong way to fidget, for best results, grab the center bearing with your thumb and forefinger, and spin it down with your other hand. Try twisting it around to feel crazy gyroscoping effects!
